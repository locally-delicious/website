Grant Process

Priority will be given to proposals that incorporate the goals of Locally Delicious and meet some or all of the following criteria over the short or longer term:

Education: Educates children and adults about how to improve personal health, community health, and the health of the planet through the food choices we make.
Equity: Helps provide healthy local organic food to all in the community, not just the wealthy.
Environment: Mitigates agricultureâ€™s impact on climate change and helps built a sustainable local food system.
Collaboration and partnerships with like-minded organizations.

Grant size:

Up to $500 per application, with some exceptions; up to eight requests will be funded each year.

Deadlines:

We have two grant cycles per year:
Spring Deadline: February 1
Fall Deadline: November 1
Applicants will be notified of our decision within one month of the deadline dates above.

Completion of Project:

Grantees are required to submit a brief written report and pictures to Locally Delicious with the outcome and lessons learned.
